Tears of Awakening

These aren't tears of sadness; they are tears of profound relief. The "sacrifice" (giving up his destructive ego) has resulted in flying above the darkness. It's the joy of realizing the painful death of the old self has led to a beautiful new life.

"When you are with me, I'm free"

The great paradox of the Christian faith is that true freedom is only found through complete submission to Jesus. The world tells you that freedom is doing whatever you want, but that usually leads to addiction and emptiness.

Past Simple vs. Present Perfect

Past Simple

Used for a completed action at a specific, finished time in the past. (The old life).

Verb + ed (or irregular)
  • Dylan released a gospel album in 1979.
  • Cash struggled with drugs yesterday.

Present Perfect

Used for an action in the past that has a strong connection to the present. (The resurrected life).

have/has + Past Participle
  • He has transformed his life.
  • They have found grace recently.
